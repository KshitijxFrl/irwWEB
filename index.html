DATASET / IRONWILL_V1

Template-built math data

The dataset is split into different math categories. Each category has its own templates, and those templates include chain-of-thought style steps with small variations in the reasoning and wording.

From those templates, the dataset was expanded into more than 1M data points across arithmetic, algebra, calculus, and trigonometry.

THREMINENT / C++ CUDA FRAMEWORK

Threminent

Threminent is a lightweight GPU-based training framework built with C++ and CUDA. The goal was to keep it quick to hop on, easy to extend, and direct enough to deliver a working LLM training loop without hiding the moving parts.

It handles tensors, CUDA kernels, model layers, optimizer steps, checkpoints, training assembly, and inference in one small codebase.
